WIND CURFEW 9:00am. WAIVER Rwy for TDZ slope. If other than CALM winds, DO NOT LAND! CALM winds defined as Indicated by "dead" or slight movements of the WS . Sudden Very STRONG winds possible including tailwinds, updrafts, downdrafts and rolling action on medium to short final. Other wind indicators at KIW include; turbulence at Kiwi pass, flag at KP, smoke in the valley, and winds at KWR.
